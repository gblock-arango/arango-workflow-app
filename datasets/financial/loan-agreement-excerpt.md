**CREDIT AGREEMENT**, dated as of March 15, 2025, by and among **NORTHRIDGE BEVERAGES, LLC**, a Delaware limited liability company (the "Borrower"), **NORTHRIDGE HOLDINGS, INC.**, a Delaware corporation (the "Parent Guarantor"), the lenders party hereto from time to time (the "Lenders"), and **MERIDIAN TRUST BANK, N.A.**, as administrative agent (the "Administrative Agent").

## Section 1. Definitions

- **"Applicable Margin"** means, with respect to any Loan for any day, the rate per annum set forth in the Pricing Grid corresponding to the Consolidated Leverage Ratio in effect on such day.
- **"Consolidated EBITDA"** means, for any period, Consolidated Net Income for such period *plus*, to the extent deducted in computing Consolidated Net Income, (a) Consolidated Interest Expense, (b) provision for income taxes, (c) depreciation and amortization, and (d) non-cash restructuring charges not to exceed $15,000,000 in any fiscal year.
- **"Consolidated Leverage Ratio"** means, as of any date of determination, the ratio of (a) Consolidated Total Debt as of such date to (b) Consolidated EBITDA for the most recently ended Test Period.

## Section 2. The Facility

The Lenders severally agree, on the terms and conditions set forth herein, to make revolving credit loans (each a "Revolving Loan") to the Borrower from time to time during the Availability Period in an aggregate principal amount not to exceed **$425,000,000** (the "Revolving Commitment"). The Revolving Commitment shall terminate on the fifth anniversary of the Closing Date (the "Maturity Date").

## Section 6. Affirmative Covenants

So long as any Commitment or Loan remains outstanding, the Borrower shall:

1. **Financial Statements.** Deliver to the Administrative Agent, within 60 days after the end of each fiscal quarter, unaudited consolidated financial statements of the Parent Guarantor and its Subsidiaries, certified by a Responsible Officer.
2. **Compliance Certificate.** Deliver concurrently with each set of financial statements a certificate of a Responsible Officer (i) stating that no Default has occurred and is continuing and (ii) setting forth computations demonstrating compliance with the financial covenants set forth in Section 7.
3. **Insurance.** Maintain insurance against casualty loss, liability, and business interruption with responsible insurance carriers in such amounts as are customary for companies similarly situated.

## Section 7. Financial Covenants

1. **Maximum Consolidated Leverage Ratio.** The Borrower shall not permit the Consolidated Leverage Ratio, as of the last day of any fiscal quarter, to exceed **4.00 to 1.00**.
2. **Minimum Consolidated Interest Coverage Ratio.** The Borrower shall not permit the ratio of Consolidated EBITDA to Consolidated Interest Expense, for any Test Period, to be less than **3.00 to 1.00**.

## Section 8. Events of Default

Each of the following shall constitute an "Event of Default":

- Failure to pay any principal of any Loan when due.
- Failure to pay interest within five Business Days of the date due.
- Any representation or warranty made by the Borrower proves to have been incorrect in any material respect when made.
- Failure to comply with any financial covenant set forth in Section 7.
- Commencement of an Insolvency Proceeding by or against the Borrower or the Parent Guarantor.

Upon the occurrence of any Event of Default, the Administrative Agent may, and at the request of the Required Lenders shall, declare the Loans to be immediately due and payable.
