**MASTER SUPPLY AGREEMENT** (the "Agreement"), entered into as of January 6, 2026, by and between **Cascade Electronics Manufacturing, Inc.**, a California corporation ("Buyer"), and **Zhong Lin Components (HK) Limited**, a Hong Kong limited company ("Supplier").

## 2. Products and Specifications

Supplier shall manufacture and sell to Buyer the printed circuit board assemblies ("PCBAs") set forth in Exhibit A (the "Products"), each conforming to the engineering drawings and material specifications referenced therein. Each drawing carries a revision number; Products shipped against a purchase order must conform to the drawing revision in effect at the time the purchase order was issued.

## 3. Forecasting and Purchase Orders

Buyer shall provide Supplier with a rolling 26-week forecast of expected Product demand, updated weekly. The first 8 weeks of each forecast are firm; weeks 9–13 are flexible within ±15%; weeks 14–26 are non-binding planning horizons. Purchase orders issued against the firm window shall be acknowledged by Supplier within two (2) Business Days of receipt.

## 6. Quality

### 6.1 Incoming quality

Supplier shall maintain an outgoing defective parts per million ("DPPM") rate no greater than **500 DPPM** measured on a rolling 90-day window. DPPM shall be computed as (number of Products failing Buyer's incoming inspection ÷ number of Products delivered) × 1,000,000.

### 6.2 Traceability

Each Product shall carry a serialized barcode identifying the production lot, manufacturing date, production line, and inspector identifier. Supplier shall retain production records for a minimum of **seven (7) years** and shall make them available to Buyer within 10 Business Days of a written request.

### 6.3 Corrective action

Upon identification of a systemic defect, Supplier shall deliver an **8D corrective action report** within 10 Business Days. Permanent corrective actions shall be implemented within 30 Business Days, unless a longer period is mutually agreed in writing.

## 9. Pricing and Payment

Prices are set forth in Exhibit B and are firm for the first twelve months following the Effective Date. Thereafter, prices may be adjusted semi-annually based on documented changes in copper, tin, and PCB laminate input costs, provided that no single adjustment may exceed **7%**. Payment terms are **Net 60** from the date of Supplier's invoice, payable by wire transfer in United States Dollars.

## 12. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach upon 60 days' prior written notice, if the breach remains uncured at the end of that period. Buyer may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon 180 days' prior written notice, in which event Buyer shall purchase any finished Products and unique work-in-process produced in reliance on a firm purchase order.
